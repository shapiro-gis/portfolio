Width Analysis

Project Info:

As development expands into wildlife habitats, the configuration of buildings—not just their presence—can limit animal movement. To address this, I helped develop a novel GIS method and web-based tool that measures the narrowest space, or "width," between buildings across a landscape. This metric helps identify where development may create barriers to movement, especially for migrating or wintering ungulates.

Project Details:

  • Client:Wyoming Game and Fish Department, Univeristy of Wyoming
  • Technologies:Python, HTML, CSS, C++, JavaScript, SQL, ArcGIS Pro, Postgres
  • URL:www.wildlifemovetools.org/width-calculator
